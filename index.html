17 Feathers Tattoo

Secwepemc First Nations Owned and Operated

Taking back Indigenous art one tattoo at a time. I am a Secwepemc woman and art has always been a huge passion of mine. Connecting my culture to my art is important for me to express myself. Connecting others to their culture through my art is an even more rewarding part of my work.

About Me

I get my insipration directly from nature. Through hobbies like sailing, camping, hiking, hunting, fishing, bird watching and more; I create original hand drawn designs.
